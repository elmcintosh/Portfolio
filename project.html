Projects Portfolio

Celebrate Pink

Celebrate Pink is a non-profit organization based out of Cheyenne, Wyoming that hosts fundraising for grant money to be awarded to individuals undergoing treatment for breast cancer to help improve their quality of life.
This site was created as an informational destination for individuals to learn about and apply for this organizations grants, as well as information about upcoming fundraising events.

Rose City Recumbent Cycles

Rose City Recumbent Cycles is a Portland, Oregon based cycle shop specializing in sales and repairs of recumbent bicycles. Owners are deeply involved in the cycling scene and wanted a site which represented their shop and what they have to offer as far as their products and services.
