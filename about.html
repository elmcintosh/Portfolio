About Me

My name is Erica McIntosh and I am an aspiring web developer living in Tulsa, Oklahoma. I have been a paramedic for 6 years and have recently found the joy of front-end programming. I have always been creative minded and web development has given me a modern day outlet to create, design, and produce visually appealing applications. I have been developing for about 1 year and am always improving and refining my skills as well as learning all I can about other areas and innovations of web development.
